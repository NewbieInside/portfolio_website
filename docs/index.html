My Creative Process

All of my UI/UX and design projects are based off of a practiced formula to get the result that I am looking for.

1

Discuss The Project

Teamwork and project discussion is a MUST to get to consensus regarding technologies which will perfectly fit the client's needs.

2

Brainstorming & Concept

It is important to brainstorm your next project, therefore gathering information regarding the concept of it is pivotal.

3

UI/UX Planning

Nowadays, it is important to make 'quality' and not 'quantity' products which will be convenient and simple for users.

4

Interaction

Communicating with a client regarding the project needs is necessary at all stages of work in order to achieve desirable result.
